Konda Reddy Mopuri

I am a visiting Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, Indian Institute of Technology, Tirupati, where I work on computer vision and deep learning.

Before this, I held a postdoctoral position at the Visual Computing Group (VICO) lead by Hakan Bilen at the University of Edinburgh. I did my PhD at Indian Institute of Science, Benguluru, where I was advised by Prof. R. Venkatesh Babu. My thesis is awarded Best Doctoral Dissertation Award at the SPCOM-2020. I did my Maters at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
