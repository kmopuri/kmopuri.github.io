For IIT Tirupati students

If you are a graduating student (2022) and want to know about the projects I am offering towards your BTP or MTP, you may find a broad list below. Candidates with (i) knowlede in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, etc. and (ii) experience with frameworks such as PyTorch in implementing ML stuff would be preferred.

  • Learning from long-tailed distributions
  • Robustness in DNNs - Understanding and handling the susceptibilities of Deep NNs towards robust and interpretable AI
  • Adapting DNNs to challenging environments: Involves developing efficient methods to adapt Deep learning methods and models from laboratory to complex operating environments such as resource constrained, less or no data scenarios.
  • Interpretable AI: Involves understanding the learning and inference mechanism in the DNNs from various applications.
  • Data Engineering for Deep Learning: Investigates engineering solutions to the data related challenges in learning.
These are broad research areas. There are multiple fine-grained problems within each area suitable for various academic levels of investigation, which can be explored upon a quick discussion. Interested candidates should be experienced with ML/DL frameworks (e.g. PyTorch) and concepts. I will be more than happy to work on any interesting problem in the broad areas of ML/DL/AI/CV. Candidates with ideas should encourage themselves to have a discussion with me.
